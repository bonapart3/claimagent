# ClaimAgent™ Executive Scope of Work

**Autonomous Claims Processing Platform for Insurance Carriers**

---

## Executive Summary

ClaimAgent™ is an AI-powered claims automation platform that transforms how P&C insurance carriers process automotive collision claims. By deploying intelligent automation from First Notice of Loss (FNOL) through settlement, carriers achieve **80%+ straight-through processing**, reduce cycle times from days to hours, and cut per-claim costs by 84%.

**Target Market**: Insurance carriers with $5M - $500M annual written premium seeking to modernize claims operations without massive IT investment.
