# ClaimAgent™ Adjuster Guide

**How ClaimAgent™ Works for Claims Professionals**

---

## Welcome

ClaimAgent™ is designed to be your partner, not your replacement. By automating routine collision claims, you can focus on what you do best: handling complex cases that require human judgment, empathy, and expertise.

This guide explains how ClaimAgent™ changes your daily workflow and what to expect.

---

## What ClaimAgent™ Does For You

### Claims That Are Handled Automatically

ClaimAgent™ processes straightforward collision claims from start to finish:

| Auto-Approved Claims | Your Involvement |
|---------------------|------------------|
| Fender benders under $2,500 | None needed |
| Clear liability (rear-end, parking lot) | None needed |
| Policy in good standing | None needed |
| No injuries reported | None needed |
| Low fraud indicators | None needed |

**Result**: These claims go from FNOL to payment in 2-4 hours without you touching them.

### Claims That Come to You

You receive claims that need your expertise:

| Escalated Claims | Why You're Needed |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Settlement over $2,500 | Requires review authority |
| Bodily injury | Medical evaluation needed |
| Total loss | Valuation review required |
| Liability dispute | Investigation judgment |
| Fraud indicators | SIU coordination |
| Coverage questions | Policy interpretation |
| Customer complaints | Relationship management |

## Key Features for Adjusters

### 1. AI-Assisted Documentation

ClaimAgent™ automatically:
- Extracts data from uploaded documents (OCR)
- Analyzes damage photos for severity and areas affected
- Pulls police report information
- Verifies vehicle details from VIN
- Checks policy coverage and limits

**What this means for you**: No more manual data entry. Information is pre-populated and verified.

### 2. State Compliance Automation

ClaimAgent™ handles:
- Comparative negligence calculations by state
- Total loss thresholds (varies by state)
- Diminished value rules (some states allow, some don't)
- Required notices and timeframes
- Specific state disclosures

**What this means for you**: You don't need to memorize 50 different state regulations.

### 3. Communication Templates

Pre-approved letters for:
- Claim acknowledgment
- Information requests
- Reservation of rights
- Approval notifications
- Denial explanations (requires your review)
- Payment confirmations

**What this means for you**: One click to send compliant communications.

### 4. Valuation Intelligence

ClaimAgent™ aggregates:
- Kelley Blue Book values
- NADA guides
- Edmunds data
- Black Book pricing
- Local market adjustments
- Mileage and condition factors

**What this means for you**: Defensible valuations with multi-source documentation.

## What You Control

### Human Authority Remains For:

| Decision | Your Authority |
|----------|---------------|
| **Denials** | AI cannot deny claims. All denials require your review and approval. |
| **Settlements Over Threshold** | Claims above $2,500 need your sign-off |
| **Total Loss Valuations** | You approve final ACV and settlement |
| **Liability Disputes** | You make the final liability determination |
| **SIU Referrals** | You decide whether to proceed with investigation |
| **Customer Exceptions** | Goodwill adjustments and special circumstances |

### Override Capability

You can override any AI recommendation:
- Change settlement amounts
- Adjust liability percentages
- Modify coverage interpretations
- Request additional investigation
- Approve despite fraud flags (with documentation)

All overrides are logged with your notes for audit trail.

## Common Questions

### "Will AI take my job?"

No. ClaimAgent™ handles the repetitive work so you can focus on cases that need human judgment. Insurance carriers need experienced adjusters for:
- Complex negotiations
- Customer relationships
- Fraud investigations
- Coverage interpretations
- Litigation support

### "What if the AI makes a mistake?"

- All claims over $2,500 require your review
- All denials require your approval
- All suspicious claims are escalated
- Complete audit trail for every decision
- You can override any recommendation

### "How do I learn the new system?"

- 4-hour initial training session
- Interactive tutorials in the dashboard
- Practice environment with sample claims
- 30-day supervised ramp-up period
- Ongoing support from team leads

### "What about my existing claims?"

- Current claims remain in existing system until closed
- New claims route through ClaimAgent™
- No double-entry required
- Gradual transition over 60-90 days
